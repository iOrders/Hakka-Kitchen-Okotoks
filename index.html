Popular Dishes at Hakka Kitchen Okotoks

Our Menu

Explore our most popular Indian, Hakka, and Nepali-style dishes, prepared with authentic spices and traditional cooking methods. Each dish is made fresh to order using premium ingredients for an unforgettable dining experience in Okotoks.

Tandoori Chicken

Succulent chicken pieces marinated in yogurt and a blend of traditional Indian spices, then cooked in a tandoor oven for that signature smoky flavor and charred perfection. This classic North Indian dish is a favorite at Hakka Kitchen, offering tender, flavorful chicken with a vibrant red hue.

Butter Chicken Poutine

A unique fusion dish that combines the best of Canadian and Indian cuisine: crispy golden fries topped with tender butter chicken pieces and rich, creamy butter chicken gravy, finished with fresh cheese curds. This innovative creation at Hakka Kitchen brings together comfort food favorites in one delicious dish.

Butter Prawn

Succulent prawns cooked in a rich, velvety butter sauce infused with aromatic Indian spices, tomatoes, and cream. This indulgent seafood specialty combines the delicate sweetness of prawns with the bold flavors of traditional Indian cuisine, creating a memorable dining experience.

Goat Saag

Tender goat meat slow-cooked with fresh spinach (saag) in a rich, spiced gravy. This traditional North Indian dish combines the robust flavor of goat with the earthy taste of spinach, creating a nutritious and deeply satisfying meal. Perfectly seasoned with aromatic spices, it's a wholesome favorite at Hakka Kitchen.

Lamb Curry

Tender lamb pieces slow-cooked in an aromatic curry sauce with traditional Indian spices, onions, tomatoes, and fresh herbs. This classic dish showcases the depth of flavor achieved through traditional cooking methods, creating a rich and satisfying meal that pairs perfectly with naan or basmati rice.

Prawn Curry

Fresh, succulent prawns simmered in a fragrant curry sauce with traditional Indian spices, coconut milk, and aromatic herbs. This coastal-inspired dish brings together the delicate sweetness of prawns with the bold, complex flavors of Indian curry, creating a rich and satisfying seafood experience at Hakka Kitchen.
