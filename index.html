Popular Dishes at Hakka Kitchen Okotoks

Our Menu

Explore our most popular Indian, Hakka, and Nepali-style dishes, prepared with authentic spices and traditional cooking methods. Each dish is made fresh to order using premium ingredients for an unforgettable dining experience in Okotoks.

Chilly Chicken

A popular Indo-Chinese fusion dish featuring crispy chicken pieces tossed in a spicy, tangy sauce with bell peppers, onions, and green chilies. This signature Hakka Kitchen specialty combines bold flavors with a perfect balance of heat and savory notes.

Chicken Tikka Masala

Our most popular dish featuring succulent chicken pieces marinated in traditional yogurt and spices, charcoal-grilled for authentic flavor, then simmered in a rich, creamy tomato-based sauce. This classic North Indian favorite showcases the perfect balance of aromatic spices at Hakka Kitchen.

Butter Prawn

Succulent prawns cooked in a rich, velvety butter sauce infused with aromatic Indian spices, tomatoes, and cream. This indulgent seafood specialty combines the delicate sweetness of prawns with the bold flavors of traditional Indian cuisine, creating a memorable dining experience.

Goat Rara

A hearty and flavorful Nepali-style specialty featuring tender goat meat cooked with minced goat in a rich, spiced gravy. This traditional dish combines bold aromatics, tomatoes, and authentic spices for a rustic, satisfying taste that's beloved in South Asian cuisine.

Lamb Curry

Tender lamb pieces slow-cooked in an aromatic curry sauce with traditional Indian spices, onions, tomatoes, and fresh herbs. This classic dish showcases the depth of flavor achieved through traditional cooking methods, creating a rich and satisfying meal that pairs perfectly with naan or basmati rice.

Butter Chicken with Naan

The ultimate Indian comfort food combination: tender chicken pieces in a silky, tomato-based butter sauce with aromatic spices, served with freshly baked naan bread. This beloved dish perfectly balances creamy richness with subtle spices, making it a favorite at Hakka Kitchen Okotoks.
