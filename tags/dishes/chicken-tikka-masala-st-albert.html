About Our Chicken Tikka Masala

Chicken Tikka Masala is one of India's most beloved dishes and a signature specialty at Tandoori Fuzion. Our authentic recipe begins with chicken marinated in yogurt and spices, then cooked in our traditional tandoor oven to lock in flavor and juiciness. The chicken is then simmered in a rich, aromatic curry sauce made with onions, tomatoes, and a carefully balanced blend of spices including garam masala, turmeric, and coriander.
