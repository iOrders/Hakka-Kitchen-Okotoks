About Our Prawn Curry

Our Prawn Curry showcases the finest seafood preparation techniques inspired by coastal Indian cuisine. Large, fresh prawns are carefully selected for their quality and freshness, then simmered in a fragrant curry sauce made with coconut milk, tomatoes, onions, ginger, garlic, and a blend of aromatic spices including turmeric, coriander, cumin, and curry leaves. The coconut milk adds a creamy richness that balances the bold spices, while the fresh herbs enhance the natural sweetness of the prawns. The result is a dish that's both rich and light, with the prawns maintaining their delicate texture while absorbing the complex flavors of the curry. This coastal-inspired dish is perfect for seafood lovers seeking an authentic Indian dining experience.

At Hakka Kitchen, we take pride in preparing authentic coastal Indian dishes using traditional cooking methods and the finest ingredients. Our Prawn Curry is a testament to our commitment to quality and authenticity. Perfect for dine-in, takeout, or delivery throughout Okotoks.
