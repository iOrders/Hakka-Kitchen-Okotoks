About Our Chicken Tikka Masala

What makes our Chicken Tikka Masala exceptional is the authentic preparation method. The chicken is first marinated for hours in a blend of yogurt, ginger, garlic, and traditional spices, then cooked in our tandoor for that signature charred flavor. The sauce is prepared fresh daily with tomatoes, cream, butter, and a secret blend of spices that has been perfected over years. This classic dish represents the best of North Indian cuisine and is perfect for both spice lovers and those new to Indian food.

At Hakka Kitchen, we take pride in preparing authentic North Indian dishes using traditional cooking methods and the finest ingredients. Our Chicken Tikka Masala is a testament to our commitment to quality and authenticity. Perfect for dine-in, takeout, or delivery throughout Okotoks.
