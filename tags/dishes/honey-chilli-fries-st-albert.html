About This Dish

What Makes Our Honey Chilli Fries Special

Our Honey Chilli Fries are a perfect example of Indo-Chinese fusion cuisine. We start with perfectly crispy french fries, then toss them in our house-made honey chilli sauce that combines the sweetness of honey with the heat of chilli peppers. The addition of stir-fried bell peppers, garlic, and ginger adds depth and texture, while a garnish of sesame seeds and spring onions provides the perfect finishing touch.

What makes our Honey Chilli Fries special is the perfect balance of flavors - sweet, spicy, and savory all in one dish. The fries remain crispy even after being tossed in the sauce, making them the perfect appetizer or side dish to share.
