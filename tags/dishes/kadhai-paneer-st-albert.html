About Our Kadhai Paneer

Kadhai Paneer is a beloved North Indian dish and a vegetarian specialty at Tandoori Fuzion. Our authentic recipe features fresh, locally-sourced paneer (cottage cheese) and crisp bell peppers cooked in a traditional kadhai (Indian wok) with aromatic spices. The dish gets its distinctive flavor from freshly ground kadhai masala, which includes coriander seeds, red chilies, and other aromatic spices.
