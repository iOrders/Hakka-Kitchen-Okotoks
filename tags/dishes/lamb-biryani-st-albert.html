About Our Lamb Biryani

Lamb Biryani is a royal delicacy and one of India's most celebrated dishes. At Tandoori Fuzion, our authentic recipe begins with premium lamb marinated in yogurt and aromatic spices. The meat is then layered with long-grain basmati rice and slow-cooked in the traditional dum style, allowing the flavors to meld perfectly. Each grain of rice is infused with the essence of saffron, mint, and our special blend of biryani spices.

What makes our Lamb Biryani special is the perfect balance of flavors and textures â€“ tender, juicy lamb, fragrant rice, and a harmonious blend of spices. The dish is garnished with crispy fried onions, fresh mint, and served with raita (yogurt sauce) on the side, making it a complete meal that's perfect for any occasion.
