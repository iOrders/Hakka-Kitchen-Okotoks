Frequently Asked Questions

Where is Tandoori Fuzion located in St. Albert?

Tandoori Fuzion is located at 1115 St Albert Trail #360, St. Albert, AB T8N 7X6. We're conveniently situated near St. Albert Centre, making us easily accessible from all parts of the city.

Do you offer delivery in St. Albert?

Yes, we offer delivery throughout St. Albert and surrounding areas. Our delivery service covers all major neighborhoods including Erin Ridge, Grandin, Lacombe Park, Downtown St. Albert, and more.

What are your most popular dishes?

Our most popular dishes include Butter Chicken, Chicken Tikka Masala, Lamb Biryani, Kadhai Paneer, and Honey Chilli Fries. Our Gulab Jamun is also a customer favorite for dessert.

Do you have vegetarian and vegan options?

Yes, we offer a wide variety of vegetarian dishes including Shahi Paneer, Kadhai Paneer, Malai Kofta, and many more. We also have vegan options available - just ask our staff for recommendations.

What are your hours of operation?

We are open Monday to Friday from 11:30 AM to 9:00 PM, and Saturday to Sunday from 12:00 PM to 9:00 PM.

How can I contact you?

You can reach us by phone at +1 (780) 459-4808 or email at info.tandoorifuzion@gmail.com. You can also order online through our website.
