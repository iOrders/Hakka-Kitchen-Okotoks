Our Journey

A Passion for Authentic Indian Flavors

"Every dish tells a story of our homeland, crafted with recipes passed down through generations."

Welcome to Tandoori Fuzion, where we bring authentic Indian cuisine to the heart of St. Albert, Alberta. Our journey began with a simple passion: to create exceptional Indian dishes that capture the essence of traditional flavors while embracing modern fusion techniques.

From our carefully crafted recipes featuring signature dishes like Butter Chicken, Chicken Tikka Masala, and mouthwatering Lamb Biryani, to our warm hospitality, every aspect of Tandoori Fuzion is designed to transport you to the rich culinary heritage of India.
